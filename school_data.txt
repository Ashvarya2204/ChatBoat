School Name: Modern Public School
Established: 1995
Location: Main Road, City Center
Our school is a premier educational institution dedicated to providing quality education and holistic development of students.

Classes Offered: We offer classes from Nursery to Class 12th with both CBSE and State Board curriculum. Our curriculum is designed to meet international standards while maintaining Indian cultural values.

Facilities: Our school has world-class facilities including well-equipped science laboratories for Physics, Chemistry, and Biology, modern computer labs with latest technology, library with 10000+ books and digital resources, spacious sports ground for cricket and football, Olympic size swimming pool, modern auditorium with 500 seating capacity, and smart classrooms with interactive boards in all classes.

School Timings: School starts at 8:00 AM and ends at 2:00 PM for all classes. Office hours are from 9:00 AM to 4:00 PM on all working days. Saturday is a half day with classes from 8:00 AM to 12:00 PM.

Admission Process: Admissions are open throughout the year for all classes. Interested parents should visit the school office with required documents including birth certificate, previous school transfer certificate and marksheet, Aadhar card of student and parents, address proof, and recent passport size photographs. Admission forms are available at the school office or can be downloaded from our website. An entrance test is conducted for classes 6th onwards.

Fee Structure: The fee varies by class and includes tuition, examination, and activity fees. Nursery to Class 5: Rs 25000 per year. Class 6 to 8: Rs 30000 per year. Class 9 to 10: Rs 35000 per year. Class 11 to 12: Rs 40000 per year. Fee can be paid in quarterly installments. Sibling discount of 10% is available.

Transportation: We provide safe and comfortable bus facility covering all major areas of the city with 15 different routes. All buses are GPS tracked and have lady attendants. Bus fees range from Rs 8000 to Rs 12000 per year depending on distance. Bus timings are coordinated with school hours.
